Procedure


Step 1

 Construct a wooden rectangular prism using wooden planks and a dowel.

Step 2

 The vinyl sheet flooring, linoleum flooring, and luxury vinyl tile will be cut to size

Step 3

 Place a flooring material on top of the vernier force plate directly under the canonball weight. Drop the cannonball weight onto each flooring material for a total of 50 trials per material.

Step 4

 3D print and then construct an anthropomorphic head. Drop the anthropomorphic head onto each flooring material and record the acceleration-time for each collision.

Step 5

 Modify Head Injury Criterion in order to be compatible with the results of the anthropomorphic head testing
